Who We Are

LearnNova is an educational platform dedicated to providing high-quality learning resources across multiple disciplines. Founded by ROHAN GAWANDE, and SHLOK SANE .

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to make learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone. We believe that education should not be limited by location, background, or resources. By curating valuable educational content and organizing it in an intuitive way, we aim to empower learners to take control of their educational journey.
